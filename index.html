Popular Destinations

Japan

Japan, located in Eastern Asia, is a sovereign island nation known for its vibrant cities, historic temples, imperial palaces, and natural beauty. Visitors can explore neon-lit Tokyo, the cultural heart of Kyoto, the culinary hub of Osaka, and the scenic landscapes of Hokkaido and Okinawa, making it a destination that caters to all interests.

Price: 2,10,000Rs/- per Person

South Korea

South Korea is a country that seamlessly blends ancient traditions with modern advancements, offering a unique cultural experience. The country is known for its K-pop, Korean dramas, and world-famous cuisine. Visitors can enjoy the blend of centuries-old traditions with cutting-edge technology and rapid modernization.

Price:1,70,000Rs/- per Person

Thailand

Thailand is famous for its ancient temples and royal palaces, such as the Grand Palace in Bangkok, Wat Pho, and Bang Pa-in Royal Palace . Nature lovers can explore Doi Suthep-Doi Pui National Park in Chiang Mai, home to diverse flora and over 300 bird species. For a quieter experience, towns like Sangkhlaburi offer jungle tours and elephant treks.

Price:50,000Rs/- per Person

China

China is a vast and diverse country, offering a wide range of experiences for tourists. From the Great Wall of China to the Terracotta Warriors, from the Forbidden City to the modern skyline of Shanghai, China has something for everyone. The best time to visit is during the Spring (March to May) and Fall (September to October).

Price:1,65,000Rs/- per Person
